How the Impossible Burger has Impacted McDonalds

   

The following are monthly sales for the company per region:

     

Sales per region analysis:

The Impossible Burger was introduced in October 2018, which is the integral date to keep an eye on with these graphs. Let's start with looking at each region. For the most part, ever since October 2018, sales in every region have generally not gone back to the sales amount of October 2018. Aside from the Northwest USA, there hasn't been a single month where sales have sunk to or below the October 2018 sales amount. Overall sales have been able to steadily increase and remain above that October 2018 dip in profits.

Sales per item analysis:

 

Moving onto the graphs per item, the sales for each item over the past few months have been able to steadily recover. There are inevitably the routine ups and downs in sales as there have been even prior to the release of the Impossible Burger. If you look closely to these charts, you can see that sales never for each item never reached the amount of October 2018 again. This shows us that there is little need to worry about specific items --such as the hamburger-- from decreasing significantly in sales as its new competitor settles into the market.

   

Keep in mind daily sales trends:

Key Takeaways

  • Daily sales are important in order to visualize where things can be improved. In this case, it looks like sales are strongest for hamburgers generally on the weekends.
  • Overall, the Impossible Burger did initially hit McDonalds sales hard. However, sales have been able to regain a footing and slowly increase again. The Impossible Burger hasn't had as significant of an impact that it would warrant further targeted action.
  • In terms of future action, if you're continuing to look for more ways to be profitable, utilizing daily trends data would be the best place to start. Look for ways to increase sales during the week or focus more on the weekends and perhaps introduce some promotions or special deals that would drive even more customers into restaurants during the weekend. Targeted action to respond to the Impossible Burger does not seem to be necessary.
